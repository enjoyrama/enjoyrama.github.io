Rescue

Protect the body's cells — don't let the viruses reach them.

← Back to Virurama

How Rescue Works

Scattered across each stage are cells that must be kept alive. If a virus reaches one before you neutralise it, the cell is lost — reducing your score multiplier and potentially ending your run.

Cells to Protect

❤️

Red Blood Cell

+500 pts on rescue

The most common rescuable cell. Slow, vulnerable, and worth protecting at all costs.

⚪

White Blood Cell

+800 pts on rescue

A fragile ally cell — if rescued it briefly slows nearby viruses.

🔵

Stem Cell

+1500 pts + power‑up

Rare and valuable. Rescuing one awards a bonus power‑up for the next stage.

💛

Platelet

Score multiplier ×2

Rescuing all Platelets on a stage doubles your final stage score.

Tips

🛡️ Place Barriers between cells and incoming viruses at the start of each stage to buy yourself time.
🎯 Prioritise Stem Cells — their power‑up can make the next stage significantly easier.
⚠️ Losing all rescuable cells on a stage triggers a score penalty and removes a multiplier stack.
