About the Game

Virurama is a tactical arcade game set inside the human body. You control an antibody moving on a grid, using Bio‑Blocks, Antibiotics, and responsive Barriers to eliminate viruses while managing space, timing, and risk.

Core Features

• Tactical Arcade: fast-paced action with quick, decisive — and deadly — choices.
• Reactive enemy viruses that wander, chase you, and break the field.
• Bio‑Blocks, Antibiotics, and responsive Barriers to push, destroy, and combine against viruses.
• Hidden DNA bonuses and score multipliers for skilled play.
• 16 random procedural stages with increasing difficulty.
• Online high‑score leaderboard to show your best runs.
• Minimal, clean visual style focused on clarity and feedback.
