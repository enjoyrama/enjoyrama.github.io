Score Multipliers

Multipliers stack and are applied to the total stage score before being added to the run total.

×2

All Platelets Rescued

Rescue every Platelet on the stage.

×1.5

No Deaths

Complete a stage without losing any rescuable cell.

×1.5

Speed Clear

Clear the stage in under 60 seconds.

×2

Full Genome

Collect all DNA types on the stage.

×3

Flawless

No cells lost + all DNA collected + speed clear combined.

+10%

Per Stage Streak

Each consecutive stage cleared without losing a cell adds 10% to the cumulative multiplier (stacks up to ×3).
