Characters

Meet the inhabitants of the micro‑world — allies and enemies alike.

← Back to Virurama

Allies

🦠

Antibody

Ally · Player

The protagonist. A mobile antibody that navigates the grid, deploys tools, and eliminates viruses.

🧱

Bio‑Block

Ally · Tool

Solid biological matter you can place to obstruct virus movement or trigger chain reactions.

🛡️

Barrier

Ally · Tool

A temporary wall that absorbs virus hits before breaking down.

Enemies

☣️

Wanderer Virus

Enemy · Basic

Moves randomly across the grid. Low threat alone, but dangerous in swarms.

⚡

Chaser Virus

Enemy · Aggressive

Actively pursues the antibody. Faster and more dangerous than the Wanderer.

💣

Breaker Virus

Enemy · Disruptor

Destroys Bio‑Blocks and Barriers on contact. Tears apart your defensive setup.

✏️ More characters and variants will be revealed as the game approaches release.
