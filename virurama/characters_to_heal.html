Characters to Heal

During the hard 16 stages trip, you are inside the human body of a particular character that you can collect in your trophy gallery inside the game.

← Back to Virurama

Unlock Charactersfor each human body trip completion

Characters have only the meaning of a trophy and are artwork/social representative. Each month new characters will be added by the development team. When you win a character, the next trip will be set with a random available character to heal. Please look at the online Trophies Leaderboard to see your rank on Virurama's best player list.
